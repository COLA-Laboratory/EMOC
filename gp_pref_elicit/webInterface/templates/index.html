Preference Elicitation Study

Supporting Multi-Objective Decision Making.

Thanks for taking the time to participate in this study, in which we explore ways of supporting humans in complex decision problems.

In such a scenario, there are many options with multiple attributes to choose from, but not enough time (or patience) to evaluate them all. The goal is to find the best option as quickly as possible.

You'll be asked to answer different types of questions, from which the algorithm infers your preferences and starts making better suggestions.

Note: Javascript has to be enabled, and the experiments do not work on mobile.
